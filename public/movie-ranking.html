The Best Movies According To Sam!

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Magadheera

Raghuveer murders Indira's father and blames Harsha. When Harsha sets out to his clear his name, he recalls his previous life from 400 years ago and his connection to them.

100% Love

Balu and Mahalakshmi fall for each other but go their separate ways due to ego clashes. Years later, when their grandmother falls ill, they are forced to confront each other.

S/O Satyamurthy

After the death of his billionaire father, a young man awakens to the harsh reality of his fortune. To complicate matters further, he falls in love with a hardened creditor's daughter.
